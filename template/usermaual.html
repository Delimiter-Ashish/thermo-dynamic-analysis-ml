Instructions for Admins

As an admin, you have access to the admin form where you can manage data generation, Training And Testing. After logging in, navigate to the Data Generation page to upload your CSV file. This will allow you to generate the necessary training and testing files.

Once training is complete, hitting the "Test" button will log you out and redirect you back to the analysis page. You can then perform new training and receive updated responses based on the new data. Ensure that the data is accurate and formatted correctly to achieve the best results.

Model Information and Data Processing

Our analysis platform utilizes a Random Forest model to predict thermodynamic properties such as specific heat capacity (Cp), enthalpy (H), and entropy (S). The Random Forest algorithm is chosen for its robustness and accuracy in handling complex datasets.

To ensure the model receives meaningful inputs, we have implemented one-hot encoding for the chemical symbols and specie IDs. One-hot encoding is a process that converts categorical data into a format that can be provided to machine learning algorithms to improve their performance.

This method allows the model to understand the categorical variables effectively, ensuring that each chemical symbol and specie ID is accurately represented during the training process.
